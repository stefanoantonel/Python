Our Privacy Policy

When you browse S.O.S. Math......

Our website's logfile will save the following information about your visit: Your internet provider's IP address, time and date of your visit and the pages visited. The logfiles do not contain any information which allows to identify you personally.

When you use our CyberBoard.......

The CyberBoard uses cookies to store the date of your last visit and your preferences. The database will record the information you submit when you register. You are not required to use your real name, only your real email address! The database will also store your internet provider's IP address, time and date a message was sent, and your messages themselves. We will not disclose any of this information to third parties, unless required by law. We may use your email address to send you an occasional newsletter keeping you updated about our company and its products.

When you order one of our products......

Our database will record the information we solicit on the order form, including your name, and address. All the information we solicit will be deleted after three years. We will not disclose any of this information to third parties, unless required by law. We may use your email address to send you an occasional newsletter keeping you updated about our company and its products.
We do not have access to your credit card information; all credit card information is handled by Authorize.net, a leading payment authorization service for online businesses.

When you send us email.......

Our database will record your email address, time and date the message was sent, and the message itself. We will remove this information three years after you last contacted us. We will not disclose this information to third parties, unless required by law. We may use your email address to send you an occasional newsletter keeping you updated about our company and its products.

Third Party Information.......

 We allow third-party companies to serve ads and/or collect certain anonymous information when you visit our web site. These companies may use non-personally identifiable information (e.g., click stream information, browser type, time and date, subject of advertisements clicked or scrolled over) during your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services likely to be of greater interest to you. These companies typically use a cookie or third party web beacon to collect this information. To learn more about this behavioral advertising practice or to opt-out of this type of advertising, you can visit networkadvertising.org.
